Implement src/flatten.py with a function flatten(d, sep=".") that flattens a
nested dictionary into a single-level dictionary. Nested keys are joined by sep.
Lists and other non-dict values are kept as-is (do not recurse into lists).

Example:
  flatten({"a": 1, "b": {"c": 2, "d": {"e": 3}}})
    -> {"a": 1, "b.c": 2, "b.d.e": 3}
  flatten({"a": {"b": 1}}, sep="/") -> {"a/b": 1}
